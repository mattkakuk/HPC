## **How I Help Homeowners Choose the “Right” White (Because Not All Whites

### **Warm White vs. Cool White**

Warm whites lean buttery or creamy and feel cozy. Cool whites feel crisp,
modern, and clean.

## **Choosing Durable Materials for White Kitchen Cabinets (Because Life
Happens)**

White cabinets are gorgeous… but they also need to hold up to real life—kids,
pets, cooking, coffee splashes, and the occasional “oops.”

### **Solid Wood vs. MDF vs. Plywood**

  * Solid wood is strong and classic
  * MDF is smooth and paints beautifully
  * Plywood is stable and resists warping

#### **What I Recommend Most Often**

I usually guide homeowners toward **paint-grade durable options** that balance
longevity with budget—because the goal is “beautiful for years,” not
“beautiful for months.”
