  1. **What is the best material for kitchen cabinets in a humid climate?**  
**Wood** with a good sealant or moisture-resistant MDF are ideal for humid

durability.

  2. **Are quartz countertops heat-resistant?**  
Yes, quartz countertops are heat-resistant but not heatproof. Use trivets to
protect the surface from hot pans.

  3. **How can I customize cabinets to fit my unique kitchen layout?**  
Custom cabinets allow tailored dimensions and designs. Contact us for
solutions in **Pompano Beach** or neighboring areas.

  4. **What colors are trending for countertops in 2024?**  
Neutral tones like gray and beige are timeless, but bold colors like navy and
emerald green are gaining popularity.

  5. **How do I choose the right cabinets and countertops for my coastal home?**
