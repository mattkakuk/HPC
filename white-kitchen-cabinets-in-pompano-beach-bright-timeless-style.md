**Choosing the Right Shade of White (Yes, There’s More Than One)**

Not all whites are the same, and choosing the wrong white can make a kitchen

**Bright White vs. Soft White vs. Warm White**

  * **Bright white:** crisp and modern, great for contemporary kitchens
  * **Soft white:** slightly muted, easier on the eyes, very popular for resale
  * **Warm white:** creamier tone that pairs well with wood floors and warm lighting

In **Pompano Beach** homes, lighting matters. A white that looks perfect in a
showroom can change under your kitchen’s bulbs and sunlight—so we always
factor that in when helping you choose.

**Pro Tip: Match White Cabinets to Your Lighting Temperature**

Warm lighting can make whites look creamier. Cool lighting can make whites
look icy. We like to pick a cabinet white that looks good in the morning sun
_and_ at night when the pendants are on. Because let’s be honest—most of us
live in our kitchens after dark.

**3) Should I choose bright white or warm white cabinets?** **

** Yes. White kitchens tend to photograph well, appeal to buyers, and make
