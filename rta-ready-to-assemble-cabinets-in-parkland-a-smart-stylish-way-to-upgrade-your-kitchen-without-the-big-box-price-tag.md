**What Are RTA Ready-to-Assemble Cabinets?**

RTA stands for **ready-to-assemble** —meaning the cabinets arrive flat-packed
and get assembled on-site. But don’t confuse “assemble” with “cheap.” Modern
RTA cabinetry is engineered for strength and consistency, often using tight
joinery that creates a rigid, long-lasting cabinet box.

RTA cabinets are ideal when you want:

  * A faster cabinet delivery timeline
  * A more predictable budget
  * Great style options (yes, even the “designer” looks)
  * Solid construction that doesn’t feel disposable

At Half Price Cabinets, we guide you through the whole process so you’re not

**#### RTA vs. Stock vs. Semi-Custom vs. Custom Cabinets**

Let’s simplify the cabinet world:

  * **Stock cabinets:** limited sizes and styles, often “one-size-fits-most.”
  * **RTA cabinets:** shipped flat, assembled locally, usually a better value for quality.
  * **Semi-custom:** more options, more cost, longer lead times.
  * **Custom:** maximum flexibility, maximum budget.

RTA is the “best bang for the buck” lane—especially when you work with a team
