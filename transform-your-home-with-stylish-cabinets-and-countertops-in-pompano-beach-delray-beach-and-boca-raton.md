Your countertop choice influences both appearance and practicality. Here’s a
breakdown of the leading materials.

Quartz countertops are a favorite among homeowners in Pompano Beach for their

cabinetry.

Granite and quartzite offer **natural beauty and exceptional durability** that

classic yet luxurious look.

## **Maintenance and Care for Cabinets and Countertops**

Proper care extends the life of your investment.

### **Cleaning Tips for Different Materials**

  * **Quartz and granite:** Use mild soap and water; avoid abrasive cleaners
  * **Wood cabinets:** Dust regularly and use wood-specific polish
  * **Laminate:** Wipe spills immediately to prevent staining

### **Long-term Upkeep**

Regular sealing of natural stone counters, gentle cleaning routines, and
avoiding excessive heat can keep your surfaces looking new for years.
