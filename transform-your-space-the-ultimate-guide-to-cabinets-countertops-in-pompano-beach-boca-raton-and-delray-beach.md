## **Understanding Cabinet Styles and Materials**

### **Popular Cabinet Styles in Pompano Beach and Surrounding Areas**

  * **European Cabinets** : Minimalist, sleek, and modern; perfect for contemporary homes.

## **Choosing the Right Countertops for Your Home**

### **Material Choices: Quartz, Granite, and Quartzite**

  * **Quartz** : Engineered, low-maintenance, and available in numerous colors. Ideal for busy households.
  * **Granite** : Natural stone known for its unique patterns and durability.
  * **Quartzite** : Exotic, high-end, with natural veining—perfect if you’re aiming for a luxurious look.

### **Color Selection and Finish Options**

Whether you’re leaning toward a sleek white quartz or a bold black granite,
the finish (polished, honed, leathered) affects both appearance and
maintenance.
